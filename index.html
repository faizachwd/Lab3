Toronto Fire Services Incidents 2014

This map displays the locations of emergencies that TFS responded to in 2014. Incidents that had emergency alert levels >= 1 were inlcuded, since level 0 is the initial response for any event. Hover over each point to see the number of people rescued. If you click on a point, the map will display all other incidents at alert level. Click the reset button to show all the points on the map
